About

What I'm Building

Leveling the Playing Field

Small contractors are the backbone of construction, but they're often left behind when it comes to software and technology. While large firms have entire teams dedicated to project management systems, estimating tools, and data analytics, small contractors are stuck with spreadsheets, paper, and gut instinct.

I'm working on solutions that use AI to give small contractors the same capabilities as enterprise-level operations — without the enterprise-level cost or complexity.

The goal is simple: help small contractors work smarter, bid more accurately, manage projects more efficiently, and compete on a level playing field. Technology should be an equalizer, not a barrier.
